How does this work?
Workflow

Mailtrack Hunter scans your inbox periodically, pre-filters commercial emails by header (free), extracts image URLs, then asks Claude to classify unknown ones as potential trackers. Candidates appear here for your review before anything touches the extension.

Candidate actions
  • Pending — found by Claude, waiting for your review
  • Approve — confirms the domain is a real tracker; it will appear in both exports
  • Reject — marks it as a false positive; excluded from exports but kept as history
  • Reset — moves an approved or rejected candidate back to Pending
Export buttons
  • Export full JSON — all approved candidates with full metadata (domain, source email, Claude's reasoning, timestamp); useful for auditing or feeding back to Claude
  • Export rules.json — approved domains formatted as declarativeNetRequest rules, ready to merge into Block Mailtrack's rules/rules.json
Toolbar
  • Scan now — triggers an immediate scan without waiting for the next scheduled interval
